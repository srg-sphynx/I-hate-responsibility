Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data Collection & Storage

I Hate Responsibility does not collect, transmit, or sell any of your personal data. We don't have servers, databases, or analytics tracking you.

Your excuse history is saved entirely on your device using a technology called localStorage. This means the data lives in your browser and never touches our non-existent servers.

2. API Usage

When you click the button to generate an excuse, a request is sent to the Blame as a Service API. Please refer to their repository for any of their API usage policies.

3. Clearing Your Data

You can clear your history at any time by clicking the "Clear All" button in the app, or by clearing your browser's site data/cookies. You can also decline the initial consent banner, which will prevent any data from being saved locally.
